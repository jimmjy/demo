About

Juliet is an independent creative agency founded by Ryan Spelliscy, Sarah Stringer, and Denise Cole. In past lives, Ryan worked at a variety of top shops, including Goodby Silverstein & Partners. At 36, he was named chief creative officer of JWT. It’s a post that made him one of the youngest people to ever run a network agency. Sarah held incresingly senior roles with agencies, including BBH New York, and, most recently, she held the top consumer insights job at Toronto consulting firm Jackman Reinvents. Denise spent time art-directing and designing in San Francisco, and she was awarded three D&AD pencils and two Cannes lions in the last year alone. Susan Holden is Juliet's Chief Financial Officer. She’s a two‐time Emmy nominee and former member of the Hillary For America Finance Committee. She’s not pictured. But we lover her dearly.
